So you have some great stuff you’ll like to share with the community? We are glad you are taking the journey with us a step higher, because Naspire is about sharing knowledge and helping members be more productive and successful. We are delighted to have you drive the conversation by contributing relevant and quality content to the blog and in community groups, by creating endless flow of user-generated shareable content that will benefit the entire Naspire community.

What we’re looking for

Our goal is to help community members and our readers get valuable tips, insights and information to solve problems.

Here’s all you have to do

  1. Write your best piece. We value expert opinions and contributions from thought-leaders.
  2. Pay attention to grammar usage, proof-read your write-ups and spell-check before sending it. A wrong tense, a misplaced punctuation, wrong spellings or even using discrete instead of discreet may discourage the reader from reading on.
  3. There are varieties of content types you can develop; articles, blog posts, e-books, templates, checklists, comparison chart, case study, testimonials, podcast, video, webinar, sponsored content, polls, interviews, micro videos, resource page, media mentions, memes, gifs, quotes, news releases, illustrations, giveaways, book summaries, screenshots, quiz, product review, stories, social media posts, tools, timelines, newsletter, competitions, slideshare, spreadsheet, events, link pages, info-graphics, photo galleries and the list is endless.
  4. If you are being paid (monetary or other forms of consideration) to endorse or share a product, service or opinion, you need to disclose that you are being paid for such actions or opinions. Not doing this may lead to such activity being stopped by Naspire amongst other consequence management actions.
  5. Content promoting harassment, hate-speech, racism, religion, politics, ethnicity are not welcome on the site.

How to begin

Choose a category for the blog post you’d like to write. We have a number of categories from which you can pick.

Let your posts be within the range of 500 – 1000 words

Include an author bio in your e-mail. These should be about 2-3 sentences.

Submit your post using the blog functionality in your Profile section
