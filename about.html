Naspire was founded to bridge the knowledge gap that exists among students, professionals & entrepreneurs in Africa.
It was conceived as a social learning platform to help students, professionals (blue and white collar) and entrepreneurs in Africa learn, share knowledge and experiences,connect, test their ideas, and access useful resources from one another to help them win in their life.

Our Vision

At Naspire, we believe that the free exchange of knowledge and experiences is key not just to the fulfillment of individual aspirations but also to solving Africa’s problems, powering its knowledge economy thus driving economic growth and development.

Our Mission

We also believe that our innovative approaches to learning will help achieve Goal 4 (Quality Education) of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)


Why is it called naspire?

Because it’s a network about helping people fulfill their career and life aspirations.


Where are naspire members located?

Naspire members are located across Africa and in other parts of the world.


What are our values

The Naspire community is strongly hinged on the core values of Professionalism, Mutual Respect, and Trust.
As a social learning platform in Africa, the Naspire community seeks to achieve a communal feeling among its members, hence we place premium on trust and mutual respect, which are essential to achieving our individual and collective goals.
